Mirror all of your notifications to VR with Zephyr.

- Security: Notifications never leave the local network.
- Simplicity: Pair once and you're good to go.
- Customization: Decide which notifications appear in VR.

Requires Zephyr desktop application, SteamVR, and an OpenVR headset.
